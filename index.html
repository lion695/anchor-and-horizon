About Us

Anchor and Horizon are dedicated to providing exceptional services and support to our community. With a focus on the importance of men's health and well-being, we aim to empower individuals and strengthen communities. We understand that as men, we face unique challenges that require specialized attention and care. We are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of the men we serve.

Statistics

Around 4,000 to 4,500 men take their own lives every year in the UK. Across the nation, men account for approximately three-quarters of all suicide deaths.
The Mental Health Foundation states that suicide remains the leading cause of death for men under the age of 50. The latest available figures from the Office for National Statistics detail the male suicide rate at 17.6 deaths per 100,000, with those aged 50 to 54 being at the highest risk. It is also the single biggest killer of men under the age of 45 in the UK. This accounts for more deaths in this demographic than other leading causes like accidents or even cancer. The high rate of suicide among young men is often attributed to a combination of societal pressures, the stigma surrounding mental health, and a reluctance to seek professional help or discuss emotional struggles.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a supportive and inclusive environment where men can find the resources and guidance they need to navigate the challenges of life. We strive to break down barriers and reduce the stigma associated with mental health, encouraging open conversations and promoting well-being.

Andy's Man Club

Our mission, put simply, is to prevent other families from going through the heartache and grief caused by suicide, by encouraging men to speak openly about their issues. We aim to achieve this through our Monday night talking groups, and through the power of awareness, sharing stories and sharing experiences.

Fathers Guidance

Fathers Guidance is a U.S.-based 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to supporting fathers experiencing parental alienation, challenging the stigma surrounding men's mental health, and promoting self-care. Dr. Matthew Peloquin founded Fathers Guidance to address a critical gap in mental health and family advocacy: the psychological impact of parental alienation on fathers.

Visit Fathers Guidance website

Stand Easy

"They are soldiers, people with dignity and pride, and they need to be treated accordingly." - Naji Malak, CEO/Founder & Practitioner. We offer specialised trauma and PTSD acupuncture treatment at the hands of someone with 40 years of experience treating military personnel and civilians. Carefully placed needles help to lift trauma and its subsequent symptoms. Our treatment is designed to respect soldiers and their space; we place no demands on patients. We believe talking about your trauma is not always necessary, vulnerable people should not be required to talk about darkness in order to heal. If you are a veteran experiencing PTSD symptoms seek our help today.
